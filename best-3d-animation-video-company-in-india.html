Our Creative Process

Step 1

Ideation and Brainstorming: The Foundation

Every great animation starts with a brilliant idea. At Vidzy, the ideation and brainstorming stage is where we lay the groundwork for your project. In this crucial phase, we collaborate with you to develop and refine ideas. This ensures the foundation of the animation is strong, innovative, and aligned with your goals. See More

Step 2

Storyboarding: Envisioning the Narrative

Storyboarding is where your story starts taking a visual shape. We create detailed storyboards to sketch out how each scene will unfold on screen, providing a clear vision of the final product. Vidzy offers two types of storyboarding:
Digital Storyboards
Utilizing digital assets and pre-made characters, these storyboards are efficient and give a quick sense of the narrative flow.
Manually Drawn Storyboards
Crafted from scratch with precision and detail, these storyboards offer a deeper, bespoke visualization of your project See More

Step 3

3D Modeling: Sculpting the Virtual World

In the 3D modelling stage, our artists meticulously sculpt every character, object, and environment. Whether it's a personality, a product like headphones or a watch, this step involves creating a highly detailed and accurate representation of the subject, transforming concepts into believable 3D models. See More

Step 4

Texturing: Bringing Models to Life

Texturing is where our models gain colour and surface details. Our texturing artists expertly map out textures onto each model, choosing the right colours, gradients, meshes, and materials. This process is pivotal in making characters and objects vibrant and lifelike, adding depth and realism to each element. See More

Step 5

Lighting: Setting the Tone and Atmosphere

Our lighting team takes over once the texturing is complete. They skillfully create lighting that complements the colour scheme and enhances the mood of each scene. From sunlight to artificial lights, every aspect is fine-tuned to ensure the environment around the character is realistic and visually stunning. See More

Step 6

Character Animation: Breathing Life into Characters

Here, our 3D animators take charge, adding movement and expression to the characters. They meticulously work on positioning, rotation, and scaling, among other aspects, to bring the characters to life. At this point, the narrative truly begins to animate. See More

Step 7

Camera Work and Rendering: Finalizing the Visuals

In the final stages, our camera and rendering experts step in. The camera team positions and animates cameras in 3D space according to the storyboard. Then, the render artists process these visuals, rendering out each frame to create the final, polished animation. This phase is critical in ensuring that the animation aligns perfectly with the storyboard and is visually captivating. See More
