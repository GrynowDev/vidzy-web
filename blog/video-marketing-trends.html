Key Video Marketing Trends To Follow in 2025

Short-Form Videos Are the Future of Social Media:

It is believed that short-form videos are the future of social media. But why? There is a need to understand the facts. The short-form content comprises content that is thick and profound yet also designed to be "snackable," meaning that it can be enjoyed within seconds or minutes. Typically comprising under 1,200 words, it also includes short-form videos (SFVs) popularized by platforms such as TikTok and Instagram, where users would create and share brief videos on various explanations—such as entertainment, lifestyle, fashion, food, fitness, travel, and more.

In the last few years, several short-form video (SFV) platforms in the growing creator community in the country, like YouTube Shorts, Tiktok and Instagram Reels have only sped up this development by introducing new features, monetization options, and algorithms that increase the creators' reach which has also led to the establishment of a vibrant creator community.

According to a RedSeer Strategy Consultants analysis, the short-form video (SFV) market in India is close to a moment of monetization breakthrough. The SFV market could be as big as US$ 8-12 billion by 2030 due to, among other things, a surge in the usage of smartphones. Furthermore, it predicts that by 2030, the virtual gifting market on short-form video platforms in India will grow to US$ 1.7 billion.

The statistics mentioned above give a direct indication of the significance of short video formats. This is why SFV can be assumed to be one of the best video marketing trends in 2025.

The Rise of Long-Form Videos in a Short-Form World:

Long-form videos are growing more popular because they provide extensive information and depth, attracting viewers who want more than just ordinary content. They are different from short videos in that they enable creators to fully engage with the topic, supplying useful insights and context. This extensive exploration of the subject makes them suitable for audiences who want a relevant part of their knowledge deepened, leading to more involvement and trust in the process. As a result, long-form videos are a means of building the authority of the information presented and forming tighter relationships between the audience and the producer.

Some popular formats of long-form videos are:

  • Explainer Videos: 91% of people are keeping their information about a product or service through an example video.
  • Podcasts: It provides a fun structure for interviews, stories, and group discussions. It is estimated that by 2024 there will be around 464 million podcast listeners worldwide.

UGC Video Marketing:

User-generated content (UGC) has become a primary trend in video marketing, offering brands an amazing opportunity to generate faith and original interactions with their spectators. In a world where customers opt for transparency rather than fancy advertisements, UGC is an efficient way for companies to come closer to clients and seem genuine.

UGC videos have different forms, such as vlogs, tutorials, or testimonials. They can either have user input on the entire content or only on specific parts such as comments on products.

79% of the people surveyed stated that the UGC has a significant influence on their purchase decisions.

Videos in Email Marketing:

The advent of videos in email marketing can also prove to be the best video marketing strategy. Because more than 80% of marketers use video as a marketing effort. Video in email marketing is used by marketers because it can increase engagement, improve click-through rates, and make content unforgettable. Videos quickly capture attention so they can be particularly effective in communicating key messages and driving email action.

Warby Parker, an eyewear company, used a video email campaign to advertise its new summer collection. The video was designed in such a way as to put forth the new collection as something fun and engaging, with models jumping and dancing around the place. The results of the email were a remarkable 12% increase in sales and a 19% jump in click-through rates.

Grammarly, a writing tool, employed a video email campaign to advertise their mobile application. The video elucidated how the application was able to assist users in facilitating their on-the-go writing skills, using a combination of graphics and real-life video as a means of conveying the message. This campaign increased in-app installations by 10%.

Creating Immersive Brand Experiences with AR and VR:

One of the next big video marketing secrets is the integration of augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR). These technologies make it possible for brands to create highly interactive and engaging environments that go beyond traditional video formats. In AR and VR, consumers can interact with products or spaces in real-time, such as by virtually "trying on" clothing or remotely touring a property, which enhances engagement and connection. The larger-scale rollout of AR and VR technology makes it easy for brands to be more creative and use AR or VR to enhance the brand experience and create greater customer loyalty.

Influencer Videos:

One of the top video marketing trends to Watch in 2025 That You Can’t Ignore is influencer collaboration, with the growth of brands' dependency on influencers. Through them, brands can connect with their niche audience more authentically. By collaborating with influencers, they can build trust and credibility and produce content that is a natural fit for followers.

The estimation of India's influencer marketing industry is projected to expand at a compound annual growth rate of 18% from the years 2022 through 2026 to the value of INR 3,375 crore (equivalent to USD 404.82 million), based on what is reflected in an Ernst and Young (EY) report.

For example, Nike fosters an authentic image of its brand through partnerships with athletic experts and fitness influencers. Fenty Beauty on the other side has made use of beauty influencers of different backgrounds to make product demonstrations and increase the user-generated content. So, as influencer marketing continuously discovers new horizons, brands can enhance their reach, engagement, and trust if they cleverly are allied with the influencers.

Video Marketing in E-Commerce:

Video marketing in e-commerce has indeed changed into one of the most efficient methods to attract consumers and boost sales. With the assistance of different types of content like product demonstrations, tutorials, and customer videos, companies can present all the features and advantages of their products live, making it easy for consumers to make their purchasing decisions.

For instance, Amazon permits the inclusion of product videos such as brand commercials while Sephora presents tutorials that illustrate how to apply the product and achieve the desired results. Video marketing is a noteworthy way of customer engagement. Additionally, it raises conversion levels faster than text and static graphics alone can provide the dynamic and entertaining shopping experience that video marketing can produce.
