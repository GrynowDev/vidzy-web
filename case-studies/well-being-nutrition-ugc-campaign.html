Well Being Nutrition’s UGC Campaign Boosts Conversion
by 11%

Objective –

We launched a UGC campaign for Well Being Nutrition to promote healthcare products. With the help of the guidelines that were provided, we created a creative campaign that would create a memorable impact for the audience.

The cooperation was truly cooperative, reflecting our commitment and resulting in a successful campaign that engaged with our consumers.

We partnered with influencers who produced captivating and imaginative videos to boost product awareness while generating user-generated content.

Strategy –

To create an alignment with the aims of the company, a video was created to emphasize the USPs of the product.

We selected some creators from the Lifestyle, Beauty, Mom, Fitness, and Nutritionist categories and assigned them different products corresponding to their niche.

We offered tailored scripts and supervised the creation of the videos. Subsequently, our internal team was assigned the responsibility to edit these videos, and the final product was delivered on time.

Campaign Deliverables –

An 11% increase in conversions and a decrease in customer acquisition cost were the outcomes of the campaign videos utilized in performance ads.
